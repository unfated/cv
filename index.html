Lane CHEN is the Founder & CEO of two Hong Kong startups — 2HEALTH AI LIMITED and Gene to Health Limited — building a multi-omics, big-data and AI-driven, full-life-cycle human-health digital twin platform: an end-to-end ecosystem integrating disease prediction, proactive prevention, and personalized health management. He holds a BSc in Biological Sciences from Fudan University and is completing his PhD in Statistical Genetics & Machine Learning at The University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Pak-Chung Sham — a world-renowned geneticist and national-level talent. His research spans polygenic risk prediction, Mendelian Randomization, and statistical-genetics methodology, with first- / corresponding-author publications in journals including Psychological Medicine and Statistics in Medicine. As a serial entrepreneur, he has led his teams to secure support from the HKSTP Incubation Program and the Greater Bay Area Youth Entrepreneurship funding, along with multiple Hong Kong innovation & entrepreneurship awards.
